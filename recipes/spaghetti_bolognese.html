SPAGHETTI BOLOGNESE

Y U M

DESCRIPTION

Slow cooked spaghetti bolognese in a reduced red-wine sauce

This is a recipe that I learned from the Michelin star chef Marco Pierre White.

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 200g tomatos
  • Red wine
  • 250g beef mince
  • 2 large onions
  • Clove of Garlic
